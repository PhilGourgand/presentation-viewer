Sync video and hide transcript

You can select whether to read the slides and the video transcript, or watch a self-playing slideshow with captions (subtitles) that is synchronized with the video.

When the checkbox is unchecked, you can scroll through the slides and the transcript, even while the video is playing.

When the checkbox is checked, the video controls which slide is shown. Subtitles corresponding to the video's sound are displayed below the slide.

Click on the video to start or pause it. Or with the keyboard: tab to the video and press the space bar.

Tip: If you don't see the slides and the video at the same time, try making your window wider.

Use your browser's back button to leave this page and go back to the talk.
